FAQ

Common questions.

What is Mobile Service OS?

Mobile Service OS is a mobile-first business operating system for mobile tire and roadside service companies. It lets operators quote jobs, log work in the field, track tire inventory, send branded invoices, request reviews, and track profit — all from a phone.

Is this for mobile tire businesses?

Yes. Mobile Service OS is purpose-built for mobile tire repair, mobile tire replacement, and roadside tire assistance operators. Every feature — from tire-size inventory to quick quotes — is designed for tire technicians working out of a van or truck.

Can I install it on my phone?

Yes. Mobile Service OS is a Progressive Web App (PWA), so you can install it directly to your phone's home screen without going through the App Store or Google Play. It works offline-friendly and updates instantly.

Does it track tire inventory?

Yes. Mobile Service OS includes a full tire inventory module with size, brand, cost, and quantity tracking. Inventory deducts automatically when you log a job, so your stock count stays accurate without manual updates.

Can technicians use it?

Yes. The Pro plan supports multiple technicians with role-based access. Each technician can log their own jobs from their phone, and every job is attributed to the technician who completed it for clean reporting.

Does it create invoices?

Yes. Mobile Service OS generates branded invoices with your business logo, customer details, line items, tax, and totals. You can send invoices by text or email directly from the job screen.

Can it send review requests?

Yes. After completing a job, you can send a review request SMS to the customer in one tap. The link routes them directly to your Google Business Profile review form.

Is it only for Florida businesses?

No. Mobile Service OS works for mobile tire and roadside operators anywhere in the United States. It was built in South Florida but is location-agnostic and ready for any market.
