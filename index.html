FAQ

Mobile tire & roadside operator questions.

What is Mobile Service OS?

Mobile Service OS is software built only for mobile tire and roadside tire businesses. It runs flat tire calls, logs roadside jobs, tracks tire inventory, sends branded invoices, requests Google reviews, and keeps every mobile tire technician on the same workflow from a phone.

Is this software only for mobile tire and roadside businesses?

Yes. Mobile Service OS is built only for mobile tire repair, roadside tire replacement, flat tire response, emergency roadside tire calls, and same day tire installation. Every screen, every field, and every workflow is designed around how tire jobs actually happen in the field.

Can I install Mobile Service OS on my phone in the field?

Yes. Mobile Service OS installs straight to the home screen on any phone your technicians use. No App Store, no Google Play, no downloads. It launches like a native app and updates instantly so your roadside crew is always on the latest version.

Does it track tire inventory by size and brand?

Yes. Mobile Service OS includes a full tire inventory module organized by tire size, brand, cost, and quantity. Stock deducts automatically the moment a tire is logged on a roadside job, and your hot sizes get flagged before you run out on a call.

Can multiple mobile tire technicians use it at the same time?

Yes. Pro accounts support multiple mobile tire technicians, each with their own login. Every flat tire call and roadside job is tagged to the tech that ran it, so revenue, jobs completed, and payment status are clear at the technician level.

Can it create invoices for roadside tire jobs on site?

Yes. After a flat tire call or tire replacement is finished, Mobile Service OS generates a branded invoice with your logo, the customer info, vehicle, tire size, labor, and total. The technician sends it by text or email before leaving the shoulder.

Does it send Google review requests after roadside tire calls?

Yes. Once a roadside tire job is marked complete, your technician taps one button to send a review request text. The customer is routed straight to your Google Business Profile while the experience is still fresh.

Is Mobile Service OS only for Florida mobile tire businesses?

No. Mobile Service OS works for mobile tire and roadside tire operators anywhere in the United States. It was built in South Florida by a working mobile tire operator, and the workflow fits any market running flat tire calls and roadside tire replacement.
